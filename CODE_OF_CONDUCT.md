# Code Of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want participation in this project to be respectful, welcoming, and constructive for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent and collaborate in good faith.
- Give constructive, specific feedback.
- Focus on the work, not personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or hateful conduct.
- Discriminatory or demeaning language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or deliberate disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Scope

This applies to project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and review comments.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or limit participation for behavior that violates this code.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the project maintainer through the repository owner channels.
